Blazing a trail to better facilities management.

Powerful, proven solutions for your FM needs

ideacode is a people-oriented software firm that develops agile technology solutions for colleges and universities with complex facilities management (FM) needs. Our name suggests our passion for combining creative thinking [idea] and smart approaches to software development [code] to help our clients tackle the most difficult FM challenges.

AERES, our easy-to-operate, web-based FM system, puts a warehouse full of drawings and data at your fingertips, along with tools to manage every space on your campus. Originally developed for North Carolina State University – where 50 departments and 800 people depend on AERES – our trail-blazing tools are now available to colleges and universities across the country.
